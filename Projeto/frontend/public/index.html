Mechanic

Our Mechanic Services

At Motor Masters, our mechanic service consists of a comprehensive range of solutions tailored to meet all your vehicle's needs. Our team of certified professionals is equipped with the latest tools and technology to provide top-notch service.

Our Services Include:

  • Routine Maintenance: Regular check-ups, oil changes, tire rotations, and fluid top-offs to keep your car running smoothly.
  • Diagnostics: State-of-the-art diagnostic equipment to accurately identify and address any issues.
  • Engine Repair: From minor tweaks to major overhauls, we ensure your engine performs at its best.
  • Transmission Services: Maintenance, repair, or replacement to ensure your transmission is in optimal condition.
  • Brake Services: Inspection, pad replacement, and fluid checks to guarantee your safety on the road.
  • Air Conditioning Repair: Keep cool with our A/C repair and recharge services.
  • Electrical Systems: Comprehensive care for your vehicle's electrical components, including battery checks and replacements.
  • Suspension and Steering: Ensure a smooth ride with our suspension and steering repair services.

We pride ourselves on delivering quality service and transparent pricing. Trust us to keep your vehicle in peak condition. Book your appointment today and experience the Precision Auto Works difference!

Painting

Our Painting Services

At Motor Masters, we take pride in transforming your vehicle with our expert painting services. Whether you need a touch-up, a complete color change, or custom designs, our skilled team ensures a flawless finish.

Our Services Include:

  • Auto Body Painting: From minor scratches to full-body repainting, we use high-quality paints and precision techniques to restore your car's original beauty.
  • Custom Paint Jobs: Want to stand out? Our artists can create unique designs, fades, or patterns that reflect your personality.
  • Color Matching: We meticulously match the new paint to your existing color, ensuring a seamless blend.
  • Clear Coat Application: Protect your paint and enhance its shine with our professional clear coat application.
  • Rust Repair and Prevention: We address rust spots, treat affected areas, and prevent further corrosion.
  • Paintless Dent Repair: For small dents, we use specialized techniques to restore the original finish without repainting.
  • Detailing and Polishing: Our finishing touch includes thorough detailing and polishing to make your car look showroom-ready.

At Motor Masters, we prioritize quality, durability, and customer satisfaction. Trust us to give your vehicle a fresh, vibrant look. Schedule an appointment today!

Cleaning

Our Cleaning Services

At Motor Masters, we believe that a clean car is a happy car. Our professional cleaning services ensure your vehicle looks its best, both inside and out.

Interior Cleaning:

  • Vacuuming: We remove dirt, dust, and debris from carpets, seats, and floor mats.
  • Dashboard and Console Wipe-Down: Keep your interior surfaces looking fresh.
  • Upholstery Cleaning: Stains and spills don't stand a chance against our upholstery cleaning techniques.
  • Window and Mirror Cleaning: Crystal-clear windows for better visibility.
  • Odor Removal: Say goodbye to unpleasant smells with our deodorizing treatments.

Exterior Cleaning:

  • Hand Wash: Gentle yet effective hand washing to protect your paint.
  • Wheel and Tire Cleaning: Shine up those rims and make your tires look new.
  • Clay Bar Treatment: Removes contaminants for a smooth finish.
  • Waxing and Polishing: Adds shine and protects your paint from the elements.
  • Headlight Restoration: Improve visibility and enhance your car's appearance.

Additional Services:

  • Engine Bay Cleaning: Keep your engine looking as good as it runs.
  • Pet Hair Removal: We tackle even the most stubborn pet hair.
  • Convertible Top Cleaning: Preserve your convertible's fabric or vinyl top.
